# Feature: Water Safety Resources

## Overview
Create educational water safety content to provide value to visitors and serve as a lead magnet for email signups.

## User Stories
- As a parent, I want to learn water safety tips to protect my children
- As a visitor, I want educational content that helps me make informed decisions
- As a business, we want to establish expertise and trust through helpful content

## Content Sections

### 1. Water Safety Tips Page (/resources or /water-safety)
- Drowning prevention basics
- Pool safety rules
- Supervision best practices
- What to do in an emergency
- Age-appropriate swimming milestones

### 2. Seasonal Content
- Summer pool safety checklist
- Pool party safety tips
- Vacation water safety (beach, lake, hotel pools)
- Winter pool maintenance safety

### 3. Educational Resources
- Signs of drowning (it's silent, not splashing)
- Layers of protection concept
- CPR basics and when to call 911
- Swim readiness indicators by age

## Lead Magnet Ideas
1. **PDF Download**: "10 Essential Water Safety Tips Every Parent Should Know"
2. **Email Series**: 5-day water safety email course
3. **Checklist**: Pool party safety checklist

## Page Structure

```
/water-safety
├── Hero: "Water Safety Resources"
├── Intro: Why water safety matters (35+ years experience)
├── Section: Essential Safety Tips (5-7 tips with icons)
├── Section: Age-Appropriate Guidelines
├── CTA: Email signup with lead magnet offer
├── Section: Emergency Preparedness
├── CTA: Book a lesson / Contact us
```

## SEO Considerations
- Target keywords: "water safety tips", "drowning prevention", "pool safety for kids"
- Local SEO: "Ormond Beach swim lessons", "Daytona water safety"
- Schema markup: Article, HowTo, FAQPage

- [ ] Water safety page created and accessible
