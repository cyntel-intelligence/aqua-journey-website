# Project Roadmap

## Objective
Add email capture functionality to the Aqua Journey Swim School website to build a subscriber list and provide visitors with valuable water safety content and updates.

## Success Criteria
- Email signup form integrated on the website
- Visitors can subscribe to receive water safety tips
- Content is engaging and educational
- Matches existing site design and branding
- No backend infrastructure required (serverless approach)
